My passion into space

Welcome to my Space Is The Place home page.

I have been interested in spaceflight all my life. Perhaps this resulted from my coming of age and developing an awareness of the larger world during the 1960's, when mankind made its first tentative steps beyond our protective Earth, decided that the Moon was the first goal, and BY GOLLY GOT THERE WITHIN THAT SAME DECADE!!! Pretty impressive for someone of my age in those years (i.e. 6 to 16), and proof to me that anything is possible. Mankind's destiny has to be in space.

- Ken R. Harman
